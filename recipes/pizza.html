Pizza recipe

Pizza is a beloved dish that originated in Italy and has become popular worldwide. It typically consists of a round, flat base of dough topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings such as vegetables, meats, and herbs.

This pizza recipe is easy to follow and yields a delicious homemade pizza that you can customize with your favorite toppings. Whether you prefer classic margherita or loaded with pepperoni and veggies, this recipe has got you covered.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your choice of toppings (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, olives)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat up.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza peel or baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  6. Add your choice of toppings evenly over the cheese.
  7. Drizzle the olive oil over the pizza and sprinkle with dried oregano.
  8. If using a pizza stone, carefully slide the pizza onto the preheated stone. If using a baking sheet, place it directly in the oven.
  9. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  10. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
