Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that consists of layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese. It's a hearty and comforting meal that's perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

This lasagna recipe is not only delicious but also easy to prepare. With its rich flavors and gooey cheese, it's sure to impress your guests and satisfy your cravings.

    Ingredients:

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat until fully cooked. Drain any excess fat.
  4. Add the marinara sauce to the cooked ground beef and simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom.
  6. Layer 4 lasagna noodles over the sauce.
