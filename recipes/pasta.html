Pasta Recipe

Pasta is a staple food of Italian cuisine that comes in various shapes and sizes. It is typically made from durum wheat flour mixed with water or eggs and can be served with a variety of sauces and toppings.

This pasta recipe is simple yet flavorful, perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a casual gathering with friends. You can customize it with your favorite ingredients to make it your own.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces of pasta (spaghetti, penne, fettuccine, etc.)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and saut√© for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add the diced tomatoes (with their juice) to the skillet. Stir to combine and let it simmer for about 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  4. Stir in the chopped fresh basil and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss to coat it evenly with the tomato sauce.
  6. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the pasta to absorb some of the sauce.
  7. Remove from heat and serve hot, topped with grated Parmesan cheese.
